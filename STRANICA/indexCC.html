Unlike other Courts, The one in Split didn't held corpses of long death warriors so they could revive them as zombies and use them in their army of Talons. The Court took in usually orphaned children from the region and trained them to become living weapons, which backfired when one of those kids fought back and destroyed the whole court in that city. That kid now goes by Crimson Crow. Crimson Crow is a vigilante which hunts down and punishes criminals and anyone associated with the Parliament of Owls. He wears a red-white suit and cape similar to the suit which Batman wears but no cowl with pointy ears. He was caught being able to copy movements of other known criminals and heroes which makes as believe he has a rare condition called Photographic Reflexes. His well known golden painted katana is his main weapon to bring justice down upon criminals, but he is also known to use other object to finish the job, most notiably pencils. As his area of operation included whole Europe, which took some time for us to find him and bring him here to our base. Soon I will have a talk with him in which I will offer him a deal he would not be able to refuse."
