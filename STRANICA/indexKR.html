In folk legends about Triglav, it is stated that people killed Prava and took the control of Earth. The other two vowed to punish the humanity by choosing their champions to do their will... and it looks like they found their champion. People who saw her usually confused her for being assosiated with Green Arrow because she used a bow and arrows as her main weapons. The most noticable feature is her unmatching colours of her eyes, with left being blue and right being green. Green vains are also visible around her right eye and cheek. From watching the camera footage when she was alone, she quite oftenly talked with herself, or how I came to conclude that she was talking to Nava and Java. whom only she can hear."
